How to Play
The Game

Standard 4-deck Blackjack. Beat the dealer without going over 21. You know the rules — here's what's different at KFC.

Payouts
Blackjack3:2
Win1:1
Insurance2:1
Shoes & Sessions

Each session runs through a set number of shoes. Standard players get 5 shoes per day. 21 Club Pass holders get 15. When you cash out, your balance is locked in — you can't reload into the same session.

Tax

A small rake is applied to winnings at each stake level — it scales up at higher stakes. Shown in your session log breakdown.

Need the full rules?

Hit, Stand, Double Down, Split — all work as standard. Splits are allowed on any matching rank. Double Down is available on any two-card total.

Stakes

Eight stake levels across two tiers. Your stake sets your chip denominations, minimum bet, and tax rate. Change it from the topbar badge between hands.

You can only move up one stake level at a time. Dropping requires you to have fallen below your peak balance at that level.
Side Bets

Optional bets placed before the deal. Tap the circles around the main bet to cycle through amounts. All side bets are independent of your main hand result.

Bonus Cards

Special cards that appear randomly in the shoe. When one lands in your hand it triggers automatically — no action needed.

Stamped Card — 1 in 17

Peek at up to 3 upcoming cards, then choose to increase your bet by up to your original wager before they're dealt.

Gold Card — 1 in 26

Grants a free Dealer Bust side bet worth 1× the table minimum. House money — no cost to you.

Scarface — 1 in 26

Instant payout of ½ the table minimum, added to your balance immediately.

Arrow Card — 1 in 26

Free double-down — no extra bet required. Land two Arrow Cards in one hand for a double-double-down.

Krueger — 1 in 26

Bust insurance: if you bust, you get back half your initial wager (side bets excluded).

Ice Card — 1 in 52

Instant payout equal to the full table minimum, added immediately.

Desert Card — 1 in 52

Full protection for that hand: any outcome that would be a loss or bust becomes a push instead.

Steel Card — 1 in 52

If you hit Blackjack with this card in hand, it pays 2:1 instead of 3:2.

Jesters — 1 in 52

Blood Jester — earns you a 2× leverage hand usable in a future round.

Plague Jester — the dealer adds an amount equal to your wager into the Plague Pot, which builds until it pays out.

Power-Ups

Purchasable upgrades that last one shoe. Buy them with loyalty points from the ⭐ panel. Standard players can hold 1 at a time; 21 Club Pass holders can hold 2.

Surrender Sally — Mid+

Lets you surrender against a dealer 10-upcard, recovering half your bet. Normally surrender isn't available at KFC.

Safety Net — Mid+

Any time you reach exactly 22 this shoe, it counts as a push instead of a bust. Stays active for the whole shoe.

Seven-Seeker — High+

Adds +2 to every Crazy 7s multiplier for the entire shoe. Example: 5:1 becomes 7:1, 25:1 becomes 27:1.

BJ Guard — Legendary+

If the dealer hits Blackjack, your hand becomes a push instead of a loss — no insurance needed.

BJ Novak — Legendary+

Your Blackjack pays 2:1 instead of 3:2 for the whole shoe. Stack it with a Steel Card for maximum effect.

Points can also be spent on tokens: 2× Leverage (⭐35), Insurance token (⭐25), All Side Bets min (⭐40), Extra shoe (⭐50 standard / ⭐30 pass).
Prediction Markets

Bet on what's going to happen — before it does. Tap MARKETS in the top-left to open the panel.

Shoe Markets — Bet Any Time

Short-window bets that reset each shoe. Examples: "Blackjack in the next 3 hands?", "Dealer busts 2+ times in the next 5 hands?". Each market shows a live tracker so you can follow progress. Odds are fixed at the moment you bet — what you see is what you get.

Whole-Shoe Markets

Bigger bets that run the length of a shoe: "Player wins 5 in a row this shoe?", "Dealer busts 15–17 times?". These lock once the shoe starts — bet before the first hand.

Season Markets

Monthly milestone bets: "Will [player] reach 1,000,000 KFC this month?". Settle at month-end — YES if the player hit the target, NO if not. Odds tighten as the month runs out.

Race Markets

First-to-reach bets between multiple players heading toward the same milestone. Multiple players, one winner.

Your Portfolio tab shows all open positions with live P&L, odds movement, and To Win amounts. Settled bets stay visible for one session for reference.
